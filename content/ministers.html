Robert Veil, Jr.
  • Pulpit Minister
  • Email: robert.veiljr@gmail.com

Robert Veil, Jr. has been a gospel preacher for over 40 years. He is a graduate of Freed-Hardeman University (A.A. and B.A.) where he served as President of the Preacher's Club and Religious Debate Club, and the University of Maryland School of Law (J.D.). He has preached for churches in Tennessee, Maryland, Pennsylvania and West Virginia, and has spoken in numerous Gospel Meetings, Seminars, Workshops and special Series. He has participated in a number of religious debates, and is a prolific writer and teacher. He and his wife, Marvina, have two grown children, Robbie and Kaitlyn. His passion is preaching the gospel, and is honored to serve as Pulpit Minister for the Central church of Christ.

Edwin Floyd
  • Associate Minister
  • Email: efloyd70@hotmail.com

Edwin Floyd has been a gospel preacher for over 50 years. He was educated at Freed-Hardeman University, Oklahoma Christian University, and West Virginia University (Masters in Education). He taught public school for 31 years, and has preached for churches in Tennessee, Georgia, Alabama, Virginia, West Virginia and Maryland. He began his work here at Central in 2003. He and his wife, LaNelle, have two grown children, Jeff and Chris, as well as 10 grandchildren. Among his other work, he leads in ministering to our seniors, and is beloved by the Central congregation. He serves as Associate Minister for the Central church of Christ.
